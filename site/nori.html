Nori Board Game

Nori is a recreation of the traditional Korean game, 'Yut Nori'. In Korean, Yut translates as 'Sticks' and Nori as 'Play' or 'Game'. It's a famous game that many Korean families gather around and play during the celebration of the Lunar New Year; however, recently the popularity has been declining dramatically due to other forms of entertainment being introduced. To help bring back this fading tradition, the mission of the Nori project is to recreate a modern board game that is more suitable for both children and adults to play together.
